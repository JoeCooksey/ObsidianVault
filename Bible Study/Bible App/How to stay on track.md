# 📖 Study: [[Day 27]]
**Date:** 2026-02-16
**Scripture Reference:** [[Psalms]] 17:1-5, [[Matthew]] 19:1-15, [[Job]] 4-7

### 🔍 Observations
*What is happening in the text? Who is involved? What are the key keywords or commands?*
- Devotional: 
	- In order to stay on gods tracks:
		- Your heart (what you think)
		- Your words (what you say)
		- Your feet (the places you go)
	- [[Marriage]] is of upmost importance, [[Moses]] paved a way for divorce though
	- [[Singleness]] although not explicitly mentioned states there are advantages to both [[marriage]] and [[Singleness]].
	- [[Jesus]] says we must care for the children as they are the future.
	- [[Job]] shows how not only do you have to help others stay on gods track, but also yourself
- [[David]] prays to god stating how he has stayed in the path of god despite people bribing him the other way.
- [[Jesus]] comments on [[Moses]] divorce: 
	- Anyone who commits divorce to marry another woman commits adultery
- [[Eliphaz]] tells [[Job]] that He needs to stop believing in god since he has been led to a deep despair.
- [[Eliphaz]] states how god always performs wonders that cannot be fathomed
- [[Job]] responds to [[Eliphaz]] saying how after all he has been through, the last thing he wants is to lead his friends down the wrong path.
- [[Job]] is having a sad realization that [[God]] is charging him for all of his sins at once, and how he wants to give up everyday but pushes through.
