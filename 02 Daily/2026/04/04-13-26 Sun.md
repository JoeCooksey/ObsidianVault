### Key Takeaways

- VO2 max is single best predictor of longevity
- Improvement requires addressing specific limiting factors: aerobic power, anaerobic battery, or processing power (identified via lab testing)
- 3-day beginner program (2x Zone 2 easy + 1x long run) can improve VO2 max 10-20 points in 3 months
- Core micronutrients for improvement: iron, B12, B9 (require blood work before supplementing)
- 90% of benefits achievable at 7 hrs/week cardio; extreme volume creates trade-offs with other health domains
- Training vehicle selection matters (rowing underrated; cycling leg-limited on hard efforts)

### Applicable Actions to Implement

- Get blood work: hemoglobin, iron, B12, B9 levels
- Increase daily Zone 2 movement: 20,000+ steps via frequent 5-min walks
- Incorporate post-run static stretches (hip flexors, hamstrings, calves, glutes) 30-60 sec each
- Replace pre-run static stretching with dynamic warm-up + strides
- If running seriously: track shoe mileage, replace at 300-500 miles, maintain 2-pair rotation
- Start 3-day VO2 max program: Day 1 & 3 (30-40 min Zone 2), Day 6 (45-60 min long run, alternate easy/hard weeks)
- Book VO2 max lab test when ready for personalized programming
