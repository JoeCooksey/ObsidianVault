## Key Concepts

### From Atomic Habits (James Clear)
- **1% Rule** — 1.01^365 ≈ 37.78×; tiny improvements compound dramatically; systems > goals
- **[[Habit Loop]]** — Cue → Craving → Response → Reward; the 4-step neurological cycle behind all habit
- **[[Four Laws of Behavior Change]]** — Make it Obvious / Attractive / Easy / Satisfying; design framework
- **[[Implementation Intention]]** — "I will [Behavior] at [Time] in [Location]"; closes intent-action gap
- **[[Habit Stacking]]** — anchor new habit to existing one; borrow its neural trigger
- **[[Two-Minute Rule]]** — any new habit starts as a <2 minute entry; master showing up first
- **[[Temptation Bundling]]** — pair WANT + NEED; dopamine anticipation bleeds into necessary habit
- **[[Goldilocks Rule]]** — peak motivation at edge of ability; boredom is habits' primary enemy
- **[[Commitment Device]]** — present choice locks in future behavior; Ulysses contract
- **Never Miss Twice** — one miss is an accident; two misses is a new (bad) habit starting
- **Identity as the mechanism** — every action is a vote for the person you are becoming

### From How to Win Friends and Influence People (Carnegie)
- Desire to feel important — the deepest human craving; underpins all influence and leadership
- Appreciation vs. flattery — sincere appreciation is earned and specific; flattery is detectable and cheap
- Operant reinforcement — praise every improvement immediately; the signal must reach the person in the moment
- Identity lever — treat people as who they could be and they rise to it; give a reputation to live up to

### From Never Eat Alone (Ferrazzi)
- [[Generosity-First Networking]] — the currency of real networking is not greed but generosity; give before you need
- [[Relationship Action Plan]] — 3yr→1yr→90day goals mapped to the people you need to know; build before you need it
- [[Pinging System]] — lightweight ongoing contact maintains relationships; frequency beats intensity; birthday effect
- [[Follow-Up Formula]] — follow up within 24h; reference a specific conversation detail; give something first
- [[Super Connectors]] — people who connect everyone else; one relationship with a super connector > 20 weak ones
- [[Health Wealth Family Framework]] — help someone with health, wealth, or their children = unbreakable bond

## Cross-Domain Connections

### Atomic Habits × Neuroscience
- [[Habit Loop]] (Clear) maps directly onto [[Neuroplasticity and Habit Formation]] — cue → response repetition migrates habit from hippocampus to basal ganglia
- [[Limbic Friction]] (Huberman) is the neuroscience correlate of Clear's Law 3 (Make It Easy) — environment design reduces activation energy
- [[Two-Minute Rule]] directly targets the Limbic Friction barrier — reduce the transition cost to near zero

### Atomic Habits × Psychology
- [[Identity-Based vs Goal-Based Habits]] — both Clear and Huberman converge on this frame; Clear adds the evidence-accumulation mechanism
- [[Emotional Agility]] — values-aligned action (Susan David) pairs naturally with identity-based habits (Clear); both frame behavior as expression of self-concept
- [[Three-Phase Day Framework]] — Huberman's circadian windows are the ideal canvas for Habit Stacking chains
