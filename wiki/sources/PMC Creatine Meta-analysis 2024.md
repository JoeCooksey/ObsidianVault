# Systematic Review and Meta-Analysis: Creatine Supplementation and Cognitive Function (2024)

24 RCTs, ~1,000 participants. GRADE evidence assessment. Most comprehensive review available as of mid-2024.

**Limitation:** Heterogeneity across studies is substantial. Low-to-moderate certainty on most outcomes except memory. Larger robust trials needed.
