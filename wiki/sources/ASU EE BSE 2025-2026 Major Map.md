## Area Pathways — Need 4 (Each 4 Credits)

Each pathway is a 4-credit specialization course that branches into a technical elective domain. Students choose 4 of 7.

| Pathway Course | Name | Domain | Prerequisites |
|----------------|------|--------|---------------|
| EEE 304 | Signals and Systems II | Comm, Signal Processing & Controls | EEE 203 |
| EEE 335 | Analog and Digital Circuits | Electronic Circuits | EEE 334 |
| EEE 341 | Electromagnetic Fields and Waves | Electromagnetics | EEE 203, EEE 241 |
| EEE 352 | Properties of Electronic Materials | Physical Electronics & Photonics | CHM 114, EEE 241, PHY 241 |
| EEE 360 | Energy Systems and Power Electronics | Power Systems | EEE 202; co-req EEE 241 |
| EEE 333 | Hardware Design Languages & Programmable Logic | Computer Engineering | EEE 120, EEE 202 |
| EEE 394 | Quantum Engineering | Quantum Engineering | MAT 275, MAT 342/343 |

## Technical Elective Areas — Need 15 Credits Total

Students take 5 upper-division electives (3 credits each) within or across these areas.

| Area | Available Courses | Typical Semester |

| Electronic Circuits | EEE 425, EEE 433 | F/S |
| Electromagnetics | EEE 443(S), EEE 445(F) | F/S |
| Physical Electronics & Photonics | EEE 434, EEE 435, EEE 436, EEE 437, EEE 439, EEE 465 | F/S |
| Power Systems | EEE 460, EEE 463, EEE 465, EEE 470, EEE 471, EEE 472, EEE 473 | F/S/N |
| Computer Engineering | EEE 404, EEE 419 | F/S |

*(F = Fall, S = Spring, N = Not regularly offered)*
