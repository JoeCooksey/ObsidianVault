# IEEE Spectrum: GaN and SiC Fight for Green Tech Domination

Authoritative IEEE Spectrum piece comparing SiC and GaN competitive positioning for EV and green energy applications.

## Key Insight
Market segmentation is becoming clearer: SiC owns traction inverters and DC fast chargers (high voltage, high power). GaN is taking OBCs and bidirectional converters (high frequency, moderate voltage). The 1200V GaN threshold is the key battleground for 2025+.
