# The Cognitive-Enhancing Outcomes of Caffeine and L-theanine: A Systematic Review

Systematic review covering RCTs on the combination. High-quality source with consistent findings across studies.

**Key limitation:** Some studies used higher doses (200+ mg caffeine) than typical daily use. Generalizes well to standard 100mg caffeine + 200mg L-theanine protocol.
