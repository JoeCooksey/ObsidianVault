# Research: First Job Roadmap — ASU Freshman in Livermore, CA

**Query**: First-year ASU EE student living in Livermore, CA — effective roadmap to landing a first job in the area.

---

## Answer

See [[First Job Roadmap — Livermore Tri-Valley]] for the full actionable roadmap.

## Key Findings

### Local Employer Landscape (Livermore/Tri-Valley)
- **National Labs** (literally in Livermore): Lawrence Livermore National Laboratory (LLNL) + Sandia National Laboratories — the two most powerful employers within walking distance
- **Major tech/industrial**: Chevron (San Ramon), AT&T, Lam Research, PG&E, Topcon Positioning Systems
- **Biotech cluster** (Pleasanton, 10 min drive): 10X Genomics, Bio-Rad Digital Biology, Unchained Labs
- **200+ venture-backed startups** in Tri-Valley; $763M VC investment in 2024; Hacienda Business Park (Pleasanton) is the hub
- **Tri-Valley = rising Bay Area innovation hub** — LLNL/Sandia anchored an ecosystem; biotech + hard tech growing fast

### LLNL Internship Program
- **Engineering paid summer internship** — applications open early winter each year (Oct–Jan for following summer)
- 2026 application cycle: **closed** — target **2027 summer** (apply Oct 2026)
- Multiple tracks: Engineering, Computing, Physical & Life Sciences
- GPA minimum not strictly stated for all tracks; U.S. citizenship or permanent residency required
- URL: llnl.gov/join-our-team/careers/find-your-job/internship

### Sandia National Laboratories (Livermore site)
- **DOE Community College Internship (CCI) Program** — 10-week full-time paid summer internship
- Requirements: full-time enrollment, GPA ≥ 3.0, U.S. citizenship/LPR/asylee/refugee
- 2026 deadline was January 7 — target **2027 summer** (apply by Dec 2026)
- URL: sandia.gov/careers

### ASU Career Resources (Free)
- **Handshake**: 30,000+ jobs/internships posted annually; ASU's primary job board
- **ASU Global Virtual Internship**: Remote internship credit via Virtual Internships network (13,000+ companies worldwide)
- **Forage**: Free virtual job simulations — great for building experience without a job offer
- **ASU Career Services**: Free resume review, virtual practice interviews, advisor appointments

### Freshman-Specific Tech Programs (CS-adjacent, not EE-specific)
- **Microsoft Explore** (12-week, underclassmen-focused) — software/PM, not pure EE
- **Google STEP** — first/second-year underrepresented students
- **Uber STAR** — 12-week paid, freshmen/rising sophomores
- These are mostly CS-focused but crossover roles (firmware, embedded systems, data) may accept EE

### GitHub / Portfolio Signal
- 87% of recruiters use LinkedIn; specific headline beats generic one
- Pin 6 repos; class projects + self-study projects both count
- Contributing to open source (even docs) signals collaboration skills
- First-year GitHub portfolio outcompetes zero-portfolio sophomores in early recruiting

### LinkedIn Strategy
- Headline: "EE Student @ ASU | Python + LTspice + Embedded Systems | Seeking 2027 Summer Internship"
- Connect with ASU alumni at LLNL/Sandia/Lam Research via LinkedIn search
- Post small project updates — firms see activity as signal of initiative
