# Wiki Overview

Personal second brain. Connects ideas across multiple disciplines — engineering, mathematics, books, and deep research topics. Built to surface cross-domain connections that wouldn't be obvious inside any single subject area.

## Purpose

The goal is not just to store what was read, but to build a compounding map of how ideas relate across domains. A concept in mathematics may illuminate something in engineering. A book's argument may conflict with or support a research paper. These connections get written here permanently.

## Active Domains

- [[Engineering]] — software systems, hardware, architecture, CS theory
- [[Mathematics]] — proofs, applied math, statistics, geometry, foundations
- [[Books]] — key ideas extracted from books across all genres
- [[Research]] — deep-dive synthesis on specific research topics
- [[Theology]] — Bible study, systematic theology, apologetics, church history

## How to Use

- **Adding a source**: Drop it in `.raw/` and say "ingest [filename]"
- **Asking a question**: Just ask — Claude reads the index, finds relevant pages, synthesizes
- **Health check**: Say "lint the wiki"
- **Finding something**: Check [[Wiki Index]] or search by tag
