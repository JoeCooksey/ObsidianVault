# IEEE Power and Energy Society (PES) — ASU Student Chapter

## Overview
IEEE sub-society chapter focused on power and energy engineering. Operates within the IEEE Phoenix Section. Active on Instagram (@ieee_pes_asustudentchapter) and LinkedIn.

## Focus Areas
Power systems, energy conversion, power electronics, electric vehicles, renewable energy. Directly aligned with WBG/SiC/GaN career track.

## Benefits
- Specialized power/energy networking versus broad IEEE membership
- Access to IEEE PES global resources and publications
- Connection to power-focused ASU faculty (Prof. Raja Ayyanar — EV/PV/WBG/motor drives)
- Industry contacts in power sector and energy companies
