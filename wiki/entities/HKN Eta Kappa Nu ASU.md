# Eta Kappa Nu (HKN) — ASU Epsilon Beta Chapter

## Overview
IEEE's international honor society for electrical and computer engineering. Invitation-based; requires high academic standing. ASU chapter won Key Chapter recognition for 2020–21. Part of IEEE Phoenix Section.

## Eligibility
Invitation-based; generally top 25% of class for EE/CE students. Relevant starting junior year. Not open-access like general IEEE membership.

## Benefits
- Resume distribution directly to industry partners (rare and high-value)
- "Lunch With Professors" — structured faculty access and FURI research connections
- Informational sessions about research opportunities
- Networking with engineering professionals and faculty
- Community service (Feed My Starving Children)
- Hands-on engineering experience across multiple disciplines

## Tier
A-Tier for Joe — target for Year 2/3 when eligible; HKN's industry resume distribution is one of the most direct pipelines from classroom to employer contacts available at ASU.
