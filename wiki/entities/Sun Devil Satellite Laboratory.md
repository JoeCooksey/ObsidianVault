# Sun Devil Satellite Laboratory (SDSL)

## Overview
Student-led organization designing, building, and operating small satellites and spacecraft at ASU. Industry partnerships with Altium, Northrop Grumman, Lockheed Martin, Orbital ATK.

## Active Projects (2024–2026)
- **Coconut CubeSat** — 1U CubeSat; expected launch April 2026
- **Pulsed Plasma Thruster (PPT)** — in-space propulsion R&D
- **SquidSat** — multidisciplinary satellite development
- **ASCEND** — payload development project
- **High-altitude weather balloons** — launched each semester (fastest path to Year 1 deliverables)

## EE Relevance
- **PCB design** via Altium partnership (Altium is industry-standard EDA; appears on resumes)
- Avionics, power systems, RF communication sub-systems
- Design reviews and schedule management (industry-style milestones)
- Northrop Grumman, Lockheed Martin, Orbital ATK partnerships open aerospace networking doors

## Tier
S-Tier for Joe — PCB design experience + Altium + space industry networking; weather balloon sub-team = fastest path to real deliverables in Year 1.
