# Jensen Huang

Co-founder and CEO of [[NVIDIA]]. One of the longest-tenured CEOs in Silicon Valley. Has led Nvidia from a gaming GPU company to the dominant AI compute infrastructure company.

## Core Mental Models

| Model | Description |
|-------|-------------|
| **Electrons → Tokens** | Nvidia's job: transform electrons into tokens as efficiently as possible. "As much as necessary, as little as possible" for everything in between. |
| **Five-Layer Cake** | AI is a stack: energy → chips → compute platform → models → applications. US must win all layers. See [[AI Five-Layer Cake]]. |
| **Do as much as needed, as little as possible** | Company philosophy: do what only Nvidia can do; let partners do the rest. Guides decisions on not becoming a hyperscaler, not picking winners. |
| **Don't pick winners** | Invest in every foundation lab. Humility from Nvidia's own early architectural mistakes — "precisely wrong" initial 3D graphics architecture. |

## Strategic Positions

- **CUDA**: 20 years of commitment while losing money; created domain-specific libraries (cuLitho, cuBLAS, etc.) because "nobody else would"
- **Supply chain**: Informs and aligns CEO partners on market size; makes this investment possible because Nvidia's downstream demand is large enough to guarantee offtake
- **China**: Against aggressive export controls; argues US should compete globally across all 5 layers; warns telecom precedent where US was "policied out of the world"
- **Not a hyperscaler**: Supports neoclouds (CoreWeave, Nscale, Nebius) to invest rather than operate clouds directly

## Key Quotes

> "The input is electrons, the output is tokens. In the middle is Nvidia."

> "Don't pick winners. Either let them all take care of themselves, or take care of all of them."

> "Blackwell is 50× Hopper. Not because of transistors — transistors gave us 75%. The rest is architecture and algorithms."

> "The day DeepSeek comes out on Huawei first — that is a horrible outcome for our nation."
