# Wolfspeed

Wolfspeed (NYSE: WOLF) is the world's largest manufacturer of silicon carbide (SiC) wafers and power devices. It holds 62% market share in SiC wafers as of early 2025.

## Key Products

- **Gen 4 SiC MOSFET platform** (launched January 2025): 750V, 1200V, and 2300V classes; targets durability, efficiency, and lower system cost
- SiC modules for EV traction inverters, DC fast chargers, and industrial power supplies
- SiC wafer supply to third-party device manufacturers

## Role in EV Ecosystem

Wolfspeed is the primary upstream supplier in the SiC supply chain. Most automotive SiC device manufacturers (Infineon, onsemi, STMicro) depend on Wolfspeed or in-house SiC wafer capacity. Supply chain constraints for SiC wafers have been a bottleneck for EV production scaling.

## Market Context

WBG semiconductor market: $2.89B in 2024 → projected $22.48B by 2033 (CAGR 25.6%). Wolfspeed's wafer dominance gives it structural leverage across the entire SiC device industry.
