# Wendy Wood

Habit researcher and psychologist. Co-author (with Dennis Runger) of **"Psychology of Habit"**, published in *Annual Review of Psychology*. This review covers the cognitive and neural mechanisms underlying habit formation, including the role of procedural memory and repetition in neural circuit change.

## Key Work Referenced

- **"Psychology of Habit"** (*Annual Review of Psychology*, co-authored with Dennis Runger) — cited in [[Huberman Lab Essentials - Habit Formation and Breaking]] for the finding that each repetition of a habit produces small changes in the cognitive and neural mechanisms associated with procedural memory.

## Key Claims (from cited work)

- With each repetition of a habit, small changes occur in cognitive and neural mechanisms associated with **procedural memory**
- Procedural memory holds the specific sequence of steps required for a behavior to execute
