# Solar Devils ASU

## Overview
Cross-disciplinary, project-based club that builds a solar-powered electric car from scratch each year. Competes in the Formula Sun Grand Prix (track qualifier for American Solar Challenge) and the World Solar Challenge (1,800-mile Australian Outback race).

## Sub-teams
- **Electrical**: battery pack design, BMS (battery management system), solar panel integration
- **Mechanical**: chassis/shell design, brakes, suspension, drivetrain
- **Operations**: sponsorship, fundraising

## EE Relevance
The electrical sub-team is directly relevant to Joe's WBG/power electronics career track:
- Battery pack design = EV-level power management
- BMS = embedded systems + firmware + sensing
- Solar panel integration = MPPT (maximum power point tracking) and DC-DC conversion

## Recent Achievements
- Completed solar car chassis and first test drive (2024)
- Won first place at Change the World 2024
- Exhibited at Electrify Expo (May 2024)

## Tier
S-Tier for Joe — only project club where EE students do real power systems and EV-adjacent work; battery pack + BMS experience is a direct WBG/EV internship signal.
