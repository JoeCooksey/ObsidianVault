# Lawrence Livermore National Laboratory (LLNL)

**Type**: U.S. Department of Energy national security laboratory
**Location**: Livermore, California (on East Avenue — within city limits)
**Operator**: Lawrence Livermore National Security (LLNS) LLC for the DOE/NNSA
**Founded**: 1952
**Staff**: ~8,000 employees

## What They Do

- Nuclear security and deterrence (primary mission)
- National security science: stockpile stewardship, nonproliferation
- Energy and climate: carbon capture, fusion energy (NIF — National Ignition Facility)
- Bioscience and bioengineering
- Computing (one of the world's largest supercomputing centers)
- Advanced manufacturing, laser science, materials

## Relevance to Joe

LLNL is **5 minutes from Joe's home in Livermore**. This is the highest-leverage employer in the entire roadmap. Engineering internships pay $25–35/hr for undergraduates.

## Internship Programs

| Program | Level | Timing |
|---------|-------|--------|
| Engineering Summer Internship | Undergrad | Apply Oct–Jan; start June |
| Computing Internship | Undergrad/Grad | Apply Oct–Jan |
| Physical & Life Sciences Internship | Undergrad/Grad | Apply Oct–Jan |
| Government Funded WDTS Program | Undergrad | Apply Oct–Jan |

**Key requirements**: U.S. citizenship or legal permanent residency; enrolled in accredited program.

**Application opens**: ~October each year for following summer.

## How to Maximize LLNL Application Odds
1. GitHub portfolio (3+ repos) before applying
2. GPA 3.0+ (not always a hard filter for engineering, but matters)
3. LinkedIn connecting with LLNL employees (they actively search locally)
4. Attend any LLNL Livermore Valley Open Campus (LVOC) public events — shows up in applicant profile
5. Apply to *multiple tracks simultaneously* (engineering + computing + sciences)
