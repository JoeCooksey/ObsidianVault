# STMicroelectronics SiC

STMicroelectronics (ST) is one of the two dominant automotive SiC device manufacturers alongside Infineon. ST became famous as Tesla's exclusive supplier for the Model 3 traction inverter — the first mass-market SiC EV application (2017).

## Key Products

- **STPower 4th-generation SiC MOSFET** (September 2024): optimized for traction inverters, lower on-resistance, higher switching frequency capability
- SiC modules for OBC and DC fast charger applications
- SiC diodes for power factor correction

## Automotive Significance

Tesla's Model 3 design win established ST's automotive SiC credibility. Deep ties with major OEMs (BMW, Renault, Stellantis suppliers) make ST a critical supplier in the 800V EV transition.

## Related

Infineon (CoolSiC): the other dominant automotive SiC manufacturer, with BMW relationships and the GaN Systems acquisition expanding its WBG portfolio.
