# Raja Ayyanar

## Role
Senior Professor, ASU School of Electrical, Computer and Energy Engineering.
ASU site director: **EVSTS** — Center for Efficient Vehicles and Sustainable Transportation Systems.

## Research Focus
Broad senior-faculty portfolio spanning:
- Power electronics (dc-dc conversion, high-frequency converters)
- Electric vehicles (EV traction + OBC hardware)
- PV and wind grid interfaces
- Motor drives
- Wide-bandgap device applications
- Distribution feeder control (DER voltage management)

## Key Recent Papers

### Coordinated Inverter Control for PV Hosting Capacity (2025)
VQ-sensitivity-based coordinated controller for feeder-level PV integration.
- Method: real-time voltage-reactive-power sensitivity matrix + iterative linear optimization
- Result: 3× hosting capacity improvement on EPRI J1 feeder (8000+ nodes), no active-power curtailment
- Led by student Dhaval Dalal
- **Note**: Feeder-control paper — more relevant for grid/DER interests than hardware

### Volt-PF Control for DER-Dense Feeders (2025 IEEE Access)
Reactive-support allocation tied to both voltage and active power.
- Solves inequitable Q-support burden; PF held 0.9–1.0
- Validated on real feeder with >200% DER penetration
- Led by student Madhura Sondharangalla
