# Tau Beta Pi (TBΠ) — ASU Chapter

## Overview
Nation's second-oldest honor society, representing the entire engineering profession (all disciplines). Founded 1885 at Lehigh University. 238+ collegiate chapters. ASU chapter initiates 30+ students per semester.

## Eligibility
- **Juniors**: must rank in top 1/8 of class
- **Seniors**: must rank in top 1/5 of class
- **Graduate students**: top 1/5 of class; 50% of degree completed
- Beyond GPA: personal integrity, breadth of interests, adaptability, unselfish activity

## Benefits
- Up to 32 Fellowships of $10,000 for one year of graduate study
- ~250 Scholarships of $2,000 for senior year
- Graduate School & Corporate Recruiting Fairs
- Job board with hundreds of postings
- Resume submission to major companies and top graduate schools
- Leadership development and networking with all engineering disciplines

## Tier
A-Tier for Joe — target for Year 3 (junior standing); fellowships can offset MS degree cost; Graduate School Recruiting Fair is a direct pathway to NC State MS-WBGS and VT CPES admissions.
