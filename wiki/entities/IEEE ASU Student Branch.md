# IEEE ASU Student Branch

## Overview
One of the oldest professional and technical student organizations at ASU. Part of the IEEE Phoenix Section (Region 6). Over 600 active members with 20 elected officers per academic year.

## Activities
- Technical workshops, industry seminars, tech talks
- "Dinner With Engineers" — speed-networking with Boeing, Honeywell, Intel, Texas Instruments engineers
- Resume building and networking coaching
- Annual banquet recognizing member achievements
- IEEE Day celebration (first Tuesday of October) with keynote and trivia

## Benefits
- Leadership development
- Access to IEEE digital library
- Direct industry networking with top EE employers
- Job opportunities via IEEE Phoenix Section network
- Free general student body membership; worldwide IEEE membership ~$32/year (student rate)

## Recognition
- Outstanding Large Student Branch of the Year 2018 (Phoenix Section)
- Multiple sectional and regional awards

## Tier
S-Tier for Joe — highest-leverage networking club at ASU for EE students; provides direct line to Boeing, TI, Honeywell, Intel recruiters; 600+ member community covers all EE specializations.
