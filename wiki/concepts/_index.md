- [[Task Bracketing]] — dorsal lateral striatum activates at habit start and end; neural fingerprint of consolidated habit
- [[Neuroplasticity and Habit Formation]] — habits are neural circuits; procedural memory migrates from hippocampus to basal ganglia
- [[Habit Strength]] — measured by context independence × limbic friction required
- [[Linchpin Habits]] — habits that make other habits easier; always enjoyable to the individual
- [[Identity-Based vs Goal-Based Habits]] — immediate checkbox vs. larger self-concept frame

- [[21-Day Habit Formation System]] — 6 habits/day, expect 4–5, assess automaticity after 21 days
- [[Habit Breaking via Replacement Behavior]] — insert positive behavior immediately post-bad-habit to remap neural circuits
