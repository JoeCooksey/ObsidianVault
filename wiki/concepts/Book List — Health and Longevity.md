# Book List — Health and Longevity

> The health books worth reading are the ones that change behavior, not just knowledge. Best read alongside the [[Biohacking Tier List]].

## S-Tier

- **Outlive: The Science and Art of Longevity** — Peter Attia — evidence-based healthspan extension; Medicine 3.0 framework; the four horsemen of chronic disease (heart disease, cancer, neurodegenerative, metabolic); exercise as the single most powerful longevity drug; ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐
- **Why We Sleep** — Matthew Walker — the science of sleep; 8 hours is the biological minimum for most; what deprivation does to every system; aligns with [[Sleep Optimization Supplements]]

## A-Tier

- **Lifespan: Why We Age and Why We Don't Have To** — David Sinclair — information theory of aging; NAD+, sirtuins, epigenetic clock; controversial on some supplement claims but foundational conceptual framework
- **The Obesity Code** — Jason Fung — insulin as the master fat-storage hormone; intermittent fasting as metabolic intervention; debunks calorie-in/calorie-out as the primary model
- **Breath** — James Nestor — the science of breathing mechanics; nasal breathing, CO₂ tolerance, sleep apnea; surprising and actionable

## B-Tier

- **The Longevity Diet** — Valter Longo — fasting-mimicking diet research; Blue Zones analysis; plant-forward eating for longevity
- **Wim Hof Method** — Wim Hof — breathing, cold exposure, and mindset; heavily overlaps with [[Biohacking Daily Health Hacks]]
