# Caffeine and L-Theanine Stack

The caffeine + L-theanine combination is the most evidence-backed acute cognitive enhancer available without a prescription. L-theanine (an amino acid in tea) modulates caffeine's stimulatory effects — preserving alertness while reducing jitteriness and anxiety.

## Mechanism

- **Caffeine**: blocks adenosine receptors → reduces fatigue signaling, increases dopamine and norepinephrine activity
- **L-theanine**: promotes alpha brain wave activity → calm alertness without sedation; partially blunts caffeine's sympathetic activation (jitters, blood pressure spike, anxiety)
- **Combined**: synergistic effect on sustained attention and reaction time; better cognitive performance than either alone in most studies

## Evidence Quality: High (for acute effects in young adults)

Strong evidence from multiple RCTs specifically in young, healthy adults:

- **Selective attention / reaction time:** Significantly improved in sleep-deprived young adults (double-blind RCT, 2025) — hit rate, target discriminability, reaction time to critical stimuli all improved vs placebo
- **Processing speed:** 27.8 ms faster visual color discrimination vs placebo (meta-analysis)
- **Stroop task performance:** Improved accuracy and reaction time in athletes and healthy adults
- **Cognitive + physical performance:** Elite curling athletes — Stroop accuracy + shooting precision improved with combined dose vs either alone

## Dosing

**Optimal ratio:** 2:1 L-theanine to caffeine

| Component | Dose | Notes |
|-----------|------|-------|
| Caffeine | 75–200 mg | Coffee = ~80–100mg; avoid >400mg/day total |
| L-theanine | 150–400 mg | Supplement (tea has small amounts, ~20mg/cup) |

**Common protocol:** 100 mg caffeine + 200 mg L-theanine (one cup of coffee + L-theanine capsule)

**Timing:** 30–45 minutes before focused work session.

## Tolerance and Cycling

Caffeine tolerance develops within 1–2 weeks of daily use. To preserve effect:
- **Cycle**: 5 days on, 2 days off; or take 1–2 days off per week — see [[Caffeine Cycling Protocol]] for full strategies including pre-competition taper
- **Avoid** caffeine after 2 PM — half-life is 5–7 hours; evening caffeine impairs sleep quality even if you fall asleep normally (see [[Caffeine Timing Protocol]])
- **Sleep is the most powerful cognitive enhancer.** Caffeine masks sleep deprivation without repairing it.

## What L-Theanine Does Alone

Without caffeine: calming without sedation, mild anxiety reduction, improved sleep quality at higher doses (200–400 mg). Useful on its own for high-anxiety exam periods or pre-sleep.

## Safety

Both compounds are generally recognized as safe. L-theanine has no known toxicity at normal doses. Caffeine risks: anxiety, elevated heart rate, dependency, sleep disruption. Respect the 400 mg/day caffeine ceiling recommended by FDA.
