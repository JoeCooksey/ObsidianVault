# ASU EE Clubs Tier List

## S-Tier — Join Immediately (Week 1)

| Club | Type | Key Value |
|---|---|---|
| **IEEE ASU Student Branch** | Networking | 600+ members; "Dinner With Engineers" speed-networking; Boeing/TI/Honeywell/Intel; free entry |
| **Solar Devils** | Project-based | Battery pack + BMS + MPPT = real power electronics; WBG-adjacent; American/World Solar Challenge |
| **Sun Devil Satellite Laboratory (SDSL)** | Project-based | Altium PCB design; Northrop/Lockheed industry ties; weather balloon sub-team = Year 1 deliverables |

## A-Tier — Join When Eligible or Time Permits

| Club | Type | Key Value |
|---|---|---|
| **IEEE PES Chapter** | Networking | Power/energy-specific networking; WBG career alignment; join alongside IEEE main |
| **HKN Eta Kappa Nu** | Honor Society | Resume sent to industry partners; faculty access; FURI pathway; target Year 2–3 |
| **Sun Devil Rocketry** | Project-based | Avionics sub-team; first liquid engine test April 2025; high-signal aerospace portfolio piece |
| **ASU Drone Club** | Project-based | Design/build/program drones; FPV + payload; embedded systems skills |

## B-Tier — Supporting Role

| Club | Type | Key Value |
|---|---|---|
| **Tau Beta Pi** | Honor Society | Top 1/8 junior GPA; $10k fellowships + grad school recruiting fair; target Year 3 |
| **Combat Ready Robotics** | Project-based | Rapid prototyping; workshops + competition cycle; lower rigor than SDSL/Solar |
| **Sun Devil Motorsports (FSAE)** | Project-based | Electrical sub-team exists; high time commitment (full race car build) |
| **AI Society / Quantum Club** | Academic | Low time commitment supplementary exposure; tangential to core EE |

## C-Tier — Optional by Identity Alignment

| Club | Type | Key Value |
|---|---|---|
| **SHPE** | Networking | Hispanic engineering professional development |
| **NSBE** | Networking | Black engineering academic and professional support |
| **Google Developer Student Club** | Academic | Software-focused peer learning; tangential to EE |

## Key Rules
1. **Depth > breadth**: 2 deep clubs beat 8 surface memberships (Jeffrey Hall time math applies here too)
2. **Week 1 = S-tier**: Join IEEE and one project club in Week 1; social/professional groups stabilize by Week 6–8
3. **Project sub-team > general membership**: Join Solar Devils *electrical* sub-team; join SDSL *PCB* or *weather balloon* team
4. **Honor society timeline**: HKN and Tau Beta Pi require GPA thresholds — earn them, don't rush

## Joe's Recommended Timeline
- **Week 1**: IEEE ASU (free, zero barrier) + Solar Devils electrical sub-team application
- **Month 1**: SDSL weather balloon team + IEEE PES Chapter (join both IEEE sub-orgs together)
- **Year 2**: Target HKN invitation (high GPA required) + IEEE PES active involvement
- **Year 3**: Tau Beta Pi if top 1/8 junior standing
