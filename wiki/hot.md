## Key Recent Facts

### Cycling Periodization (newest)
- **Annual arc (5 phases)**: Base+Strength (Oct–Dec) → Build+Power (Jan–Mar) → VO2 Max Blocks (Apr–May) → Competition (Jun–Aug) → Transition (Sep–Oct)
- **Polarized model (Seiler)**: 80% Zone 1 / ~20% Zone 3 — avoid Zone 2 "grey zone" accumulates fatigue without strong adaptation
- **Strength arc (off-season gym)**: Adaptation (2–3 wk) → Hypertrophy (4–6 wk) → Strength (4–6 wk) → Power (3–4 wk); 2–3×/week. Meta-analysis: heavy lifting improves cycling economy + TT performance.
- **VO2 max blocks**: Concentrated 2–3 week blocks, 2×/week intervals (2–5 min at 106–120% FTP). Short intervals beat long for time above 90% VO2 max. NOT done year-round — blocks after aerobic base.
- **Block structure**: 3 hard weeks + 1 recovery week per mesocycle. Do not skip recovery week.
- **Training plan created**: 6-day/week (rest Sunday), 5 phases mapped to calendar year
