# Daily Note: 2026-04-20

## 🙏 Daily Prayer

Lord, You are worthy of all praise. You are the Author of every good thing — the one who set the stars in place and still knows my name. You are faithful when I am fickle, steady when I am anxious, and present when I feel alone. Your power is not distant; it is woven into each breath, each morning, each unexpected moment of grace. I praise You not for what You give, but for who You are — immovable, loving, and true. May I carry the awareness of Your greatness into everything I do today, and may that awareness make me humble, grateful, and free. Amen.
