## 🙏 Daily Prayer

Lord, You are faithful and gracious beyond measure — Your steadfast love greets me with new mercies every morning, and I praise You for it. Thank You for the gift of this day: for health, for a mind hungry to grow, and for every quiet blessing I too often take for granted. ## 📚 Lesson of the Day

**From: Atomic Habits** — *James Clear* — "You do not rise to the level of your goals. You fall to the level of your systems." Drastic change doesn't come from one heroic effort — it comes from the compound interest of tiny, 1% improvements stacked every single day.

**From: The Psychology of Money** — *Morgan Housel* — Know when you have *enough*. Never sacrifice your Untouchables — reputation, freedom, family, friends, and happiness — for more money; these are invaluable and no amount of wealth can buy them back once lost.

**From: Never Eat Alone** — *Keith Ferrazzi & Tahl Raz* — "The currency of real networking is not greed but generosity." Success is not a solo act — it is built on a web of genuine relationships by investing in others *before* you ever need anything back.
