## 📚 Lesson of the Day

**From: Atomic Habits** — *James Clear*
"You do not rise to the level of your goals. You fall to the level of your systems." Real change isn't built by motivation or willpower — it's built by the small, repeatable systems you put in place. Never miss twice: one bad day is a slip, two in a row is the start of a new (bad) habit.

**From: The Laws of Human Nature** — *Robert Greene*
The Law of Irrationality: Rational people identify their emotions and *subtract* them from their thinking — irrational people let emotions run the show. Before reacting to anything today, ask yourself: *will this actually matter in 30 days?* If not, it doesn't deserve to control you now.

**From: The Psychology of Money** — *Morgan Housel*
Financial success is a soft skill — it's about behavior, not intelligence. There are "Untouchables" (reputation, freedom, family, happiness) that no amount of money can buy back once sacrificed. Know when you have *enough*, and never bet the Untouchables on a bigger number.
