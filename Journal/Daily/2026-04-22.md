## 📚 Lesson of the Day

**From: Atomic Habits** — *James Clear* — "You do not rise to the level of your goals. You fall to the level of your systems." Drastic change doesn't come from heroic willpower — it comes from 1% daily improvements that compound over time, and from shifting your identity first: decide who you want to be, then prove it to yourself with small wins every single day.

**From: The Psychology of Money** — *Morgan Housel* — Never sacrifice your **Untouchables** — reputation, freedom, family, and happiness — for money. The hardest financial skill is knowing when you have *enough* and stopping the goalpost from moving; the most powerful force in finance is not high returns but time, and how you *behave* with money matters far more than how much you know about it.

**From: Never Eat Alone** — *Keith Ferrazzi & Tahl Raz* — "The currency of real networking is not greed but generosity." Build your network *before* you need it by genuinely helping others with what matters most to them, follow up within 24 hours of every meaningful interaction, and never mistake glad-handing for real connection — the goal is always mutual benefit, never scorekeeping.
