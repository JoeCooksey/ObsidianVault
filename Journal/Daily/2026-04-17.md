# Daily Note: 2026-04-17

## 🙏 Daily Prayer

Heavenly Father,

**Adoration** — Lord, You are sovereign over every day, every season, and every breath. Your faithfulness stretches beyond what I can comprehend, and Your love is without condition or limit. You are worthy of all praise.

**Confession** — I confess that I too often measure my worth by what I produce or what I have, rather than resting in who You say I am. Forgive me for the moments I let anxiety about the future crowd out trust in Your provision. Renew a steady, grateful heart in me.

**Thanksgiving** — Thank You for this new day — for the clarity of a fresh morning, for the people in my life who ground me, and for the slow, quiet ways You are working things together for good even when I cannot see it.

**Supplication** — Give me wisdom to make decisions today that align with what truly matters. Help me to focus on what is genuinely enough — in work, in relationships, in ambition — and to protect the things no amount of money or success can replace: my integrity, my freedom, and the people I love. May I walk with purpose and peace. Amen.

## 📚 Lesson of the Day

**From: The Psychology of Money** — *Morgan Housel* — Know when you have enough. The hardest financial skill is keeping the goalposts from moving — chasing ever-greater wealth risks the things that are truly invaluable: your reputation, your freedom, your relationships, and your happiness. Never sacrifice your "Untouchables" for money, because those things are worth more than money ever can be.
