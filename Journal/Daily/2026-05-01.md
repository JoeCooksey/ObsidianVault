## 📚 Lesson of the Day

**From: The Psychology of Money** — *Morgan Housel*
Know when you have "enough" — the goalpost of more never stops moving on its own, so you have to stop it yourself. Your [[Untouchables]] (reputation, freedom, family, happiness) are more valuable than any financial gain and should never be sacrificed for wealth that exceeds necessity.

**From: Never Eat Alone** — *Keith Ferrazzi & Tahl Raz*
The currency of real networking is not greed but generosity — success is built on a web of genuine relationships where you invest freely in others *before* you ever need anything back. Ping your network constantly, not just when you need a favor, and follow up within 24 hours of every meaningful interaction.

**From: The Laws of Human Nature** — *Robert Greene*
Rational people identify their emotions and consciously subtract them from their thinking, while irrational people let those emotions make decisions for them. When you feel a strong emotional reaction, pause and ask: will this actually matter in 30 days? If not, let it settle before you act.
