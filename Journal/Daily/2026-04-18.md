# Daily Note: 2026-04-18

## 🙏 Daily Prayer

Lord, I come before You with a grateful heart.

**Adoration** — You are faithful, unchanging, and worthy of all praise. Your goodness is new every morning, and Your strength is my foundation when the world feels uncertain.

**Confession** — I confess the times I've chosen distraction over discipline, and comfort over growth. Forgive me for the moments I've let pride get in the way of connection and honesty.

**Thanksgiving** — Thank You for this day, for the breath in my lungs, for the people in my life who push me to be better. Thank You for the clarity and the opportunities You continue to place in front of me.

**Supplication** — Give me the courage to show up fully today — in my relationships, my work, and my habits. Help me be present, generous, and intentional. Where I am weak, be my strength.

Amen.
