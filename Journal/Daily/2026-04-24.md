## 📚 Lesson of the Day

**From: Atomic Habits** — *James Clear*
"You do not rise to the level of your goals. You fall to the level of your systems." Build your habits using the Four Laws: make them obvious, attractive, easy, and satisfying — and invoke the Never Miss Twice rule to keep momentum when life gets in the way.

**From: The Psychology of Money** — *Morgan Housel*
Know when you have "enough." Never let the goalpost keep moving, and never sacrifice your Untouchables — reputation, freedom, family, and happiness — for more money. Financial success is a behavioral soft skill, not a math problem.

**From: The Laws of Human Nature** — *Robert Greene*
Emotions cause us to act irrationally. When you feel a strong reaction, ask: "Does this actually matter in 30 days?" Rational people identify their emotions and subtract them from their decisions — irrational people let those emotions take the wheel.
