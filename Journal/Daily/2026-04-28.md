## 🙏 Daily Prayer

*Category: Adoration — Praising God for who He is*

Lord, You are worthy of all praise — the source of every good thing, the Author of wisdom, and the Giver of life. Before I had breath You knew me. Before I had a plan You had a purpose. You are constant when the world is uncertain, steady when I am anxious, and faithful long after my trust runs out. Your greatness is beyond what I can fully grasp, yet You still draw close. ## 📚 Lesson of the Day

**From: Atomic Habits** — *James Clear* — "You do not rise to the level of your goals. You fall to the level of your systems." Real change doesn't come from willpower or big ambitions — it comes from tiny 1% daily improvements that quietly compound into a completely different life. The system you build matters far more than the goal you set.

**From: The Psychology of Money** — *Morgan Housel* — "The First Rule of Compounding: Don't interrupt it unnecessarily." Getting wealthy and staying wealthy are two separate skills; the key to both is surviving long enough to let time do the heavy lifting. Consistency and patience in the market beat chasing the highest return every single time.

**From: The Laws of Human Nature** — *Robert Greene* — "Operating with a clear sense of purpose acts as a Force Multiplier, multiplying the effectiveness of every action and decision." (Law 13 — The Law of Aimlessness) Without purpose you simply drift, pulled by whoever is loudest. Look back to your childhood passions — your Primal Inclinations — to find the life's task that is uniquely yours, and break it into Goal Ladders so it stops feeling overwhelming.
