# Daily Note: 2026-04-19

## 🙏 Daily Prayer

Lord, I come before You this morning with a grateful heart. Thank You for the gift of another day, for rest, for a mind that can learn and a body that can move. You have been faithful — providing clarity when I was confused, energy when I was tired, and peace in moments of uncertainty.

I confess that I sometimes let distractions pull me away from what truly matters — from my studies, my health, and time spent in Your presence. Forgive me for the moments I've chosen noise over stillness.

I am thankful for the curiosity You've placed in me, for the privilege of being a student, and for every person in my life who encourages me to grow.

Today I ask for focus and discipline — to study with intention, to treat my body well, and to be genuinely present with the people around me. Guide my steps, sharpen my mind, and remind me that the work I do can be done as an act of worship.

Amen.
