## 🙏 Daily Prayer
*Category: Adoration*

Lord, You are faithful and good beyond anything I can fully comprehend. You are the source of every good thing in my life — my breath, my mind, my relationships, the beauty in an ordinary morning. You are not distant or indifferent; You are present, attentive, and loving. Before I ask for anything today, I simply want to acknowledge who You are: a God who is worthy of all praise, all trust, and all of my attention. May I carry a sense of Your greatness with me throughout this day, and may it put everything else in its proper place. Amen.

## 📚 Lesson of the Day
**From: The Psychology of Money** — *Morgan Housel*

The hardest financial skill is getting the goalpost to stop moving. Know when you have "enough" — not too little, but the point where the risk outweighs the reward — and never sacrifice your untouchables (freedom, family, reputation, the love of those who matter) for money, because those things are likely worth more than money ever can be.
