## 📚 Lesson of the Day
**From: The Psychology of Money** — *Morgan Housel* — There are things money can never buy: reputation, freedom, family, genuine love, and happiness. Morgan Housel calls these "Untouchables" — and the hardest financial skill isn't growing wealth, it's knowing when you have *enough* so you never risk the irreplaceable for the merely valuable. Define your untouchables before you define your financial goals.
