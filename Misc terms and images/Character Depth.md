Three Layers:
1. Deepest layer comes from genetics, this makes us predisposed to certain moods.
2. Second layer is formed from our early childhood, when we were most vulnerable.
3. Third layer is formed from habits and experiences.
4. Fourth layer is made in order to combat insecurities.
These top two layers are so deep that we have no real conscious of the behaviors they expel. Must put in a great effort in examining ones actions in order to decipher these two layers.
