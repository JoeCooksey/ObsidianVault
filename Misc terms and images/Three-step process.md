1. Recognize the bias:
	- [[Confirmation Bias]]: to counteract this try to disprove your belief.
	- [[Conviction Bias]]: Mainly used by salesman and politicians.
	- [[Appearance Bias]]: There is no correlation between positive qualities ignore these.
	- [[Group Bias]]: Find what the group is saying, but keep your opinion in tact.
	- [[Blame Bias]]: Embrace your own mistakes as this causes deeper growth.
	- [[Superiority Bias]]: Accept you are not special and your ideas could also be wrong.
2. The Inflaming Factors:
	- [[Childhood Trauma]]: Notice patterns of irrationality to identify these traumas, identify the wound in early childhood and detach yourself from it.
	- [[Sudden Gains Or Losses]]: Whenever you experience one of these step back and apply some of the other emotion.
	- [[Rising Pressure]]: Identify when stress is taking over and prevent decisions you will regret.
	- [[Inflaming Individuals]]: See through the front they are projecting and see them as ordinary people.
	- [[Group Effect]]: Don't let a group stop your personal thoughts.
3. Strategies Toward Bringing Out the Rational Self
	- Know yourself thoroughly: find patterns in your actions.
	- Examine your emotions to their roots: self asses thoughts in a journal without a filter.
	- Increase your Reaction Time: Give emotions time to settle before acting on them.
	- Accept people as facts: Everyone is different so don't try to make everyone act the same.
	- Find balance between thinking and emotion: balance curiosity and skepticism.
	- Love the Rational: Accept that rationality is a good thing.
