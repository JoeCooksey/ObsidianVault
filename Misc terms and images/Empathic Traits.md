1. The Tough Guy: Projects tough masculinity, but occasionally shows a soft side.
2. The Saint: Appear super kind to fulfill desire for power and attention. Look for people who are doing good when no one is watching vs only when others are watching.
3. The Passive-Aggressive Character: Appear very nice and friendly, but under stress tend to lash out, look for early signs like passive aggressive comments.
4. The Fanatic: Under stress become indecisive, usually have major belief switches.
5. The Rigid Rationalist: Need facts to be persuaded, don't like change.
6. The Snob: Think they are better than everyone, but in reality they are quite ordinary.
7. The Extreme Entrepreneur: Unable to listen to others, but need others to care for them.
