More important than ever to develop in the digital age, brains wired for constant social interaction.
Four components of building the skill:
1. Empathic attitude: Accept you are not perfect, each person you meet uncover as much of them as possible, instead of only focusing on your opinion hear the other person's.
2. Visceral empathy: Pay attention to the other person's mood and tone, mirror said mood and tone, don't be too obvious try to be subtle.
3. Analytic empathy: The idea that if you don't like someone you just haven't gotten to know him enough **52** (Abraham Lincoln quote). Find out what makes the other person unique, to do this ask open ended questions or begin with an admission to establish trust.
4. Empathic skill: You need to practice to perfect this skill: be alive in the moment, as you get better you can see more cues helping you throughout life.
