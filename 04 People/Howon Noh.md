## Bio

Howon Noh creates detailed educational fitness content focused on VO2 max development, running biomechanics, and sustainable athletic programming. Emphasizes the importance of individual testing and personalized training rather than one-size-fits-all programming.

## Training Philosophy

- **90-day seasonal periodization**: Rotates training focus (Winter: muscle, Spring: VO2 max, Summer: strength, Fall: HRV/resilience)
- **Testing-driven**: Advocates for VO2 max lab testing to identify limiting factors ([[aerobic power]] vs. [[anaerobic battery]] vs. [[processing power]])
- **Sustainable intensity**: Maximum 7 hrs/week cardio during VO2 max focus blocks to achieve 90% of benefits without systemic stress
- **Multi-variate health**: Rejects single-metric optimization in favor of holistic health across dimensions
