Cobalamin; essential vitamin required for red blood cell synthesis and DNA production. High-volume cardio athletes face elevated deficiency risk due to repeated impact and physiological stress. B12 deficiency impairs [[hemoglobin]] production, directly limiting [[VO2 max]] ceiling.

## Role in VO2 Max

Vitamin B12 works synergistically with [[iron]] and [[vitamin B9 (Folate)]] to build new red blood cells. Deficiency in any one of these three limits oxygen-carrying capacity regardless of training quality.

## Dietary Sources

- Eggs
- Salmon and fatty fish
- Beef
- Dairy products

## Supplementation

B12 supplementation is safe even at high doses—body has no risk of harm from excess. Only supplement if blood work confirms deficiency.

## Testing Consideration

[[Howon Noh]] recommends blood testing for athletes doing high-volume cardio to identify deficiencies before they limit training response.
