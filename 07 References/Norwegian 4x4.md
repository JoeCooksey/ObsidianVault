Elite [[VO2 max]] training protocol: 4 sets of 4-minute hard efforts with 3-minute recovery between sets. Considered one of the most effective single interval workout formats for improving [[aerobic power]] and [[VO2 max]].

## Protocol Details

- **Set structure**: 4 sets × 4 minutes hard effort
- **Recovery**: 3 minutes between sets (easy pace/walk)
- **Intensity**: Sustainably hard; not all-out sprint
- **Frequency**: Used sparingly (1-2x per month in periodized training) due to high central nervous system demand

## Training Benefits

- Trains [[aerobic power]] maximally
- Improves oxygen utilization at ceiling
- Highly time-efficient (20 minutes total including warm-up/cool-down)
- Elite athletes consistently see VO2 max gains from this stimulus

## Progression

Beginners should master foundational [[Zone 2 training]] and shorter interval formats (400m, 800m, 1-mile repeats) before adding Norwegian 4x4 to their training.
