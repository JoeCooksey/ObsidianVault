Folate; B-complex vitamin essential for red blood cell synthesis and DNA production. High-volume endurance athletes face elevated deficiency risk. B9 deficiency impairs [[hemoglobin]] production, directly limiting [[VO2 max]] ceiling improvement.

## Role in VO2 Max

Vitamin B9 works synergistically with [[iron]] and [[Vitamin B12]] to build new red blood cells carrying oxygen. Deficiency in any one of the three limits oxygen-carrying capacity regardless of training quality.

## Dietary Sources

- Lentils
- Asparagus
- Spinach
- Dark leafy greens
- Legumes

## Supplementation Strategy

When supplementing B9, use [[methylfolate]] (active form) rather than cheaper folic acid supplements, which require metabolic conversion and are harder to absorb.

## Testing Consideration

[[Howon Noh]] recommends blood work testing for athletes doing high-volume cardio before supplementing to identify actual deficiencies versus assumed need.
