Active bioavailable form of [[vitamin B9 (Folate)|Vitamin B9]]. The form your body can actually use immediately, unlike cheaper folic acid supplements which require conversion. Methylfolate supplementation produced "crazy changes" in how [[Howon Noh]] felt according to his experience.

## Supplementation Advantages

- **No conversion required**: Ready-to-use form
- **Superior absorption**: Better bioavailability than folic acid
- **Safety**: Body has no risk of harm from excess
- **Cost**: Higher than folic acid but more effective

## Why Not Folic Acid?

Most cheaper B9 supplements use folic acid, which:
- Requires metabolic conversion to be usable
- Is significantly harder to absorb
- Is less effective at the same dose

## Role in Red Blood Cell Production

[[Vitamin B9 (Folate)]] is essential for red blood cell synthesis alongside [[iron]] and [[Vitamin B12]]. Deficiency impairs [[hemoglobin]] production and limits [[VO2 max]] ceiling.
