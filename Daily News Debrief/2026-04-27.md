## Key Themes & Synthesis
The Iran conflict is the load-bearing story across every category today — it's keeping oil elevated, stalling Wall Street, pulling Russia into active diplomacy, and fraying the Lebanon ceasefire as a side effect. Watch whether Araghchi's Moscow visit produces a Russian-brokered framework or just buys Tehran more time. The OpenAI AI-phone announcement is the most strategically significant tech story: it signals that AI labs are now competing for the *hardware layer* of consumer computing, not just the software stack — a direct challenge to Apple's integrated model and a potential inflection point for Qualcomm, which has long been overshadowed by Apple Silicon. The Magnificent Seven earnings this week will determine whether the AI capex thesis still has Wall Street's confidence or whether the Iran uncertainty tips sentiment negative.
