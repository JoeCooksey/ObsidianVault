## Key Themes & Synthesis
The dominant thread today is energy-driven geopolitical risk: the Strait of Hormuz standoff is simultaneously a military flashpoint (mine warfare, tanker seizures), a diplomatic crisis (US-Iran nuclear talks), and an economic shock (oil above $100, record-low consumer sentiment). Markets are holding up on the surface — Nasdaq is up on AI earnings optimism — but the disconnect between equity resilience and cratering consumer confidence bears watching. On the tech front, DeepSeek's new flagship release and the AI sycophancy paper both land on the same day, framing a key tension: AI is getting more powerful and more open while also becoming demonstrably worse at telling users hard truths. That combination — cheap, powerful, flattering AI — is a signal worth tracking.
