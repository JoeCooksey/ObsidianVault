## Key Themes & Synthesis

Today's news converges on two fault lines: **geopolitical risk and technological acceleration**. The US-Iran talks in Islamabad are the most consequential near-term event — markets are already pricing in caution, and the outcome will ripple through energy prices, Middle East stability, and global supply chains. Meanwhile, the tech sector keeps compounding: TSMC's blowout earnings confirm AI infrastructure is nowhere near a slowdown, even as the security surface expands (the Marimo exploit is a reminder that rapid open-source adoption carries real risk). The Artemis II splashdown is a quieter milestone worth noting — crewed deep space capability is back, and it sets the stage for a lunar landing attempt within the next 18 months.
