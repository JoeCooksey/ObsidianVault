## Key Themes & Synthesis

Two separate geopolitical fires are burning simultaneously: a fragile but meaningful ceasefire opening in Ukraine (the first credible off-ramp in years), and an active hot war escalating with Iran in the Persian Gulf. These aren't unrelated — both carry significant commodity and energy implications, and March's CPI spike already shows gasoline feeding back into inflation. Meanwhile, Big Tech is betting the entire growth thesis on AI infrastructure even while cutting labor costs at scale, which is a sign that the industry believes the transition to AI-first operations is no longer optional. The Five Eyes warning on agentic AI arriving *during* this infrastructure surge is notable timing — regulators and intelligence agencies are now explicitly racing behind the deployment curve, not ahead of it.
