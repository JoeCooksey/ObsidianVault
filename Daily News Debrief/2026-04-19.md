## Key Themes & Synthesis
The Strait of Hormuz standoff is today's organizing story: it's driving oil volatility (and thereby equity rallies when it eases), explaining the Fed's caution, and sitting behind North Korea's window to act unnoticed. The market's record-high euphoria on Iran deal *optimism* — while the parties are described as still "far apart" — looks fragile; watch for a reversal if negotiations collapse. On the tech side, Snap's AI-driven layoffs and OpenAI's life-sciences policy push are two faces of the same trend: AI is visibly reorganizing labor *and* regulatory strategy simultaneously, not just research pipelines. The Blue Origin reusability milestone and quantum-AI results are quieter but durable signals that the next infrastructure layer — reusable heavy-lift rockets and quantum-classical compute — is materializing faster than the news cycle suggests.
