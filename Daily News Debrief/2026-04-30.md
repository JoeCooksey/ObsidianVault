## Key Themes & Synthesis

The Iran conflict is the load-bearing story of the day — now at Day 61, it's simultaneously driving gas prices up, reshaping Middle East shipping lanes, straining congressional support, and pulling a carrier strike group home after a $25B bill. The blockade's oil disruption is a direct feedback loop into US consumer prices, which means the war's political cost is rising alongside its financial one.

On the market side, the divergence between tech-led optimism (Alphabet, Amazon, NVIDIA all beating) and the geopolitical overhang (oil prices, Fed holding) is the key tension to watch. The OpenAI-Amazon partnership and the Musk trial together signal that the AI industry's ownership structure — who controls frontier models and how — is entering a legal and commercial reckoning that will matter for years.
