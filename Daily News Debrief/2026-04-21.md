## Key Themes & Synthesis
The dominant thread today is **geopolitical risk colliding with financial fragility**. The US-Iran ceasefire deadline tomorrow is the single highest-stakes event in markets right now — oil near $98 and equity hesitation both reflect the same bet: that a deal materializes but is not guaranteed. Apple's CEO transition is the other major story to watch; Ternus inherits a company that must urgently navigate the AI era, and markets will be closely reading his first moves. Meanwhile, Japan's megaquake advisory and the Buffett Indicator simultaneously flashing red serve as reminders that tail risks — geopolitical, geological, and valuation-based — are unusually clustered right now.
