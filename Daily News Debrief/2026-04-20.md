# Daily News Debrief — April 20, 2026

## Top Headlines

- **Japan 7.7 Earthquake + Tsunami Warning** — A magnitude 7.7 quake struck off Iwate Prefecture, triggering a 3-meter tsunami warning across Hokkaido, Iwate, Aomori, Miyagi, and Fukushima; evacuation orders issued for ~172,000 people. Japan Meteorological Agency warned of possible aftershocks of similar magnitude this week. Source: CNN / Japan Times
- **US Seizes Iranian Vessel, Tehran Vows Retaliation** — The US Navy fired on and captured an Iranian-flagged container ship in the Gulf of Oman, defying the fragile US-Iran ceasefire. Iran called it a violation and rejected fresh peace talks in Pakistan, citing Washington's "unrealistic expectations." The ceasefire deal expires Wednesday. Source: CNN / Al Jazeera
- **Iran Rejects US Peace Talks** — Iran's foreign ministry spokesman confirmed Tehran won't join new talks, accusing the US of violating the ceasefire through its naval blockade of the Strait of Hormuz since April 13. Source: MSN / Al Jazeera

- **UK Investigates Arson on Jewish Sites as Iranian Proxy Attacks** — London police are investigating a series of arson attacks on Jewish sites as potential work of Iranian proxies, with the latest causing minor damage Saturday night. Source: CNN

## Key Themes & Synthesis
