## Key Themes & Synthesis

The dominant thread today is escalation under the guise of restraint: the Iran-U.S. conflict is nominally in ceasefire but both sides are taking active military steps — blockade, seizures, airstrikes in Lebanon — while publicly signaling openness to talks. Oil at $103 and a Navy Secretary resignation in the same day suggest the market is pricing in continued conflict even as equities rally on "ceasefire extended" framing. On the tech side, the GE Vernova surge and Tesla capex announcement point to the same underlying pressure: AI compute is driving an unprecedented build-out of physical infrastructure (power, chips, robots), and the companies positioned in that hardware layer are the clear near-term winners. Watch Hormuz oil risk as the key macro swing factor for the next week — any escalation to actual shipping disruption could break the current equity rally fast.
