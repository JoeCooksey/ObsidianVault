# Daily News Debrief — May 2, 2026

## Key Themes & Synthesis

The dominant thread today is the tension between war and markets: Iran-U.S. hostilities are actively reshaping global trade (Strait of Hormuz shipping down 90%) even as equity markets hit all-time highs on Apple earnings and peace-deal rumors. The markets are pricing in a resolution that hasn't arrived yet — Trump's explicit dissatisfaction with Iran's proposal, combined with continued Lebanon ceasefire violations, means the geopolitical risk premium could reprice sharply if talks collapse. Meanwhile, Big Tech's AI capex surge (Microsoft +49% CAPEX, SoftBank's $100B Roze play) signals that the industry is betting enormous sums on infrastructure buildout — the question going forward is whether revenue growth can keep pace with that spending before investor patience runs out.
