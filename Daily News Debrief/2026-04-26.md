## Key Themes & Synthesis
The dominant thread today is **diplomatic instability meeting domestic security drama**: the White House Correspondents' Dinner shooting and the sudden collapse of US-Iran talks in Islamabad both underscore how volatile the geopolitical and domestic security environment remains, even as equity markets price in resolution. Markets are at record highs precisely because investors are betting on deals getting done — a bet that looks shakier with each failed negotiation session. Meanwhile, the science stories quietly tell a more nuanced story than headlines typically allow: fish oil may not be the universal brain booster it's marketed as, human origins are more complex than the textbooks say, and graphene is edging toward real medical applications. The gap between popular science narratives and ground-truth research is widening — worth tracking.
