## Key Themes & Synthesis

The dominant thread today is energy and strategic chokepoints: the U.S. Hormuz blockade is simultaneously a geopolitical pressure campaign against Iran and an economic wildcard, as spiking oil prices ripple into markets even as equities hold near record highs.
