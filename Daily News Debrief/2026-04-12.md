## Key Themes & Synthesis

The record-low consumer sentiment reading suggests ordinary Americans are already pricing in a prolonged conflict premium on their cost of living. Meanwhile, the tech sector is insulated in a parallel reality: AI infrastructure spending is reaching escape velocity (Amazon's $15B AI run rate, TSMC's blowout quarter), with nuclear energy emerging as the unexpected beneficiary as data center power demand forces long-term energy commitments. Hungary's election is the quieter geopolitical wildcard — an Orbán defeat would shift the EU's internal balance of power meaningfully at a moment when Western unity on Iran will matter.
