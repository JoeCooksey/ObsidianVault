## Key Themes & Synthesis
The Iran situation is the load-bearing story of the week: every major market move (record S&P, Nvidia +5%) and the EU's Ukraine aid package traces back to peace-talk optimism, yet Tehran is actively denying direct negotiations are even planned — making today's Pakistan summit a high-stakes ambiguity. Meanwhile, the AI capex supercycle shows no signs of slowing: Tesla, Cohere, Nvidia, and Intel's results all point to 2026 being the year infrastructure spending dramatically accelerates, now pulling nuclear energy (X-energy IPO) into the orbit. The Northwestern neuron breakthrough is the sleeper story — printed neurons talking to real brain cells is the kind of milestone that looks incremental until it suddenly isn't.
