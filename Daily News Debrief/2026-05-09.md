## Key Themes & Synthesis

Today's news is defined by two overlapping dynamics: fragile de-escalation and structural uncertainty. The Russia-Ukraine ceasefire and the US-Iran diplomacy are promising signals, but both are contingent — a three-day pause is not a peace deal, and Iran has not yet responded. Meanwhile, markets are at all-time highs on strong jobs data and tech earnings, yet the same tech giants driving those gains are quietly shedding tens of thousands of workers while pouring hundreds of billions into AI infrastructure — a paradox that will define the next phase of the cycle. The hantavirus outbreak and quantum computing breakthroughs are worth watching as slow-burn stories: one a potential public health test case, the other a sign that post-classical computing is closer than headlines suggest.
